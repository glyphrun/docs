# Create Account

Glyphrun uses [Privy](https://privy.io) for authentication, providing multiple sign-in options.

## Sign In Options

### Email

1. Visit [glyphrun.com](https://glyphrun.com)
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a verification code
5. Enter the code to complete sign-in

A wallet will be automatically created for you.

### Web3 Wallet

Connect your existing Web3 wallet:

1. Click **Sign In**
2. Select **Connect Wallet**
3. Choose your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.)
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

### Social Login

Sign in with your social accounts:

- **Google** - Use your Google account
- **Twitter** - Connect via Twitter/X
- **Discord** - Sign in with Discord

## Your Glyphrun Wallet

After signing in, Glyphrun creates a wallet for you:

- **Address** - Your unique wallet address on Base network
- **Balance** - Your USDC balance for running glyphs
- **Default Allowance** - $10 initial spending limit

You can view your wallet at [Account > Wallet](https://glyphrun.com/account/wallet).

## Account Features

Once signed in, you can:

- **Run Glyphs** - Execute any glyph from the marketplace
- **View History** - See all your past glyph runs
- **Manage Wallet** - Deposit funds and adjust spending limits
- **Create Glyphs** - Build and publish your own glyphs (creator mode)

## Security

Glyphrun is **non-custodial**:

- You control your private keys (via Privy)
- Funds are held in your wallet, not ours
- All transactions are on-chain on Base network
